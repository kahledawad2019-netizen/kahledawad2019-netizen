## 👋 Who Am I?

I'm **Khaled Metwalie** — a licensed pharmacist who fell in love with data.

I spent **2+ years** behind the pharmacy counter, watching real patients depend on real decisions. A misread interaction, a wrong dosage, a stockout on a life-saving drug — these aren't abstract problems. That frontline experience became my unfair advantage: I don't just build models, I understand the **domain** behind the data.

Today I'm a **Data Scientist & Applied AI** specialist, trained through the **Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI)** under the Ministry of Communications. I specialize in transforming messy, high-volume healthcare and business data into **actionable intelligence** using Python, SQL, and machine learning.

**What sets me apart:**
🔹 **Clinical domain expertise** — I know what the data *means*, not just what it *says*
🔹 **Full pipeline ownership** — from raw CSV to deployed API, database to dashboard
🔹 **Results-first mindset** — every project has real metrics, not just code

### 🛰️ HYDRA-Net — Counter-UAV Detection

> *"Detect drones faster with cascaded multimodal intelligence"*

A **cascaded multimodal architecture** for anti-drone detection achieving **90.07% accuracy** with **91.9× median latency reduction** through confidence-gated early exit. Trained on **128K RF signal segments**.

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| **Accuracy** | 90.07% |
| **F1 (macro)** | 0.9003 |
| **ROC-AUC** | 0.9812 |
| **p50 Speedup** | **91.9×** |
| **Operating Point** | 70.9% exit @ 97.9% accuracy |

**Stack:** `Python` `Deep Learning` `RF Signals` `Multi-sensor Fusion`
